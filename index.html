Spiritual Temperament Survey

Welcome to the Spiritual Temperament Survey

This survey will help you discover your spiritual temperament. It consists of 54 questions and should take about 10-15 minutes to complete.

Your spiritual temperament reflects the way you most naturally connect with God and grow spiritually.

Questions adapted from Sacred Pathways by Gary Thomas. Website by Ian Dalmas.

Instructions

  1. Read each statement carefully.
  2. Rate each statement on a scale of 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 5 (Strongly Agree).
  3. Answer honestly based on your personal experiences and preferences.
  4. Complete all 54 questions for the most accurate results.
